About Malikka

Welcome to Reflections, a space where we dive deep into the conversations that matter most. I'm Malikka, and through this podcast, I share stories, insights, and reflections on personal growth, relationships, and the beautiful complexity of life.

Each episode is an invitation to pause, reflect, and connect with yourself and the world around you. Whether you're on your morning commute or winding down for the night, I hope these conversations bring you a moment of peace and inspiration.
