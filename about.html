About Malikka

M

A Heart for Healing & Transformation

Malikka Sims is the founder and host of Reflections with Malikka, a faith-based podcast created from a personal journey of healing, restoration, and obedience to God. Through seasons of loss, growth, and deep reflection, Malikka learned that healing does not happen by accident, it happens when we are willing to pause, look inward, and invite God into the process.

With a heart for authenticity and transformation, Malikka creates spaces where honesty is welcomed, faith is centered, and healing is not rushed. Her voice carries compassion, wisdom, and a deep reliance on Christ, encouraging others to reflect on their own lives with grace and truth.

At the core of Malikka's work is a desire to see hearts restored, minds renewed, and lives transformed, not by perfection, but by surrender. She believes that reflection is often the doorway God uses to bring healing and lasting change.

The Birth of Reflections

Reflections was born in the quiet moments after the storm in the silence that came once the noise of addiction faded and the truth of who I really was began to surface.

This podcast is more than a platform. It's a mirror.

It was birthed from the ashes of my own story a life once tangled in addiction, shame, trauma, and self abandonment. I know what it feels like to be lost, to be unseen, to seek love in people, places, and things only to find more emptiness.

But God met me in the middle of my breakdown and began a healing that started not with fixing everyone around me, but with handing me a mirror.

As I looked at the women triggering me in recovery, I realized I wasn't supposed to pray them awayI was supposed to reflect. God showed me it wasn't about them. It was about me.

About what still lived beneath the surface. About the wounds I buried under survival. About the trauma I never dared to name. And in that secret place with God, with scripture and surrender and honest conversation, Reflections was born.

This podcast is a sacred space where we look inward through God's truth. Where we hold up mirrors to our pain, patterns, and purpose,and allow the Light to do what only Light can do.

It's raw. It's real. It's redemptive.

Reflections is where healing meets transformation. Where conversations with other women turn into lifelines. Where vulnerability becomes victory. Where shame dies and identity rises.

This is for every woman who's still becoming, and needs a place to reflect, to grow, to remember:

You are not your past.
You are not what you've survived.
You are not forgotten.

You are being made new.
